AI News Short Factory is a local, single-creator tool that automates the research and production of short-form news videos — then deposits them as drafts for your personal review before anything goes live.

📰

Story research

Pulls trending news, scores relevance, and surfaces the best candidates for a short.

✍️

Script generation

Produces a fact-checked script in the creator's editorial voice, ready for review.

🎬

Video rendering

Renders the final short locally using Remotion — no third-party render farm.

📥

Draft upload

Deposits finished videos into your TikTok draft inbox via the official Content Posting API.

👁️

Human review gate

Every video waits for the creator to inspect, edit, and publish manually inside TikTok.

🔒

Local-first

All data stays on the creator's machine. No cloud storage, no third-party analytics.

How it works

1

Pipeline runs on a schedule

A daily cron job triggers story research, scoring, scripting, fact-checking, and rendering — all offline on the creator's own hardware.

2

Creator authorizes via TikTok OAuth

The creator grants a scoped upload token through TikTok's official OAuth flow. The token is stored locally and never shared.

3

Video is uploaded as a private draft

The finished video is sent to the creator's TikTok draft inbox using the Content Posting API with privacy_level: SELF_ONLY.

4

Creator reviews and publishes

The creator opens TikTok, inspects the draft, edits caption/hashtags as needed, and taps Post — entirely at their discretion.

No auto-publishing

AI News Short Factory never posts content to a public audience automatically. It never schedules public posts, never changes video visibility to public, and never publishes without explicit creator action inside TikTok.
